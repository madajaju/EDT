In this episode of "Embracing Digital Transformation," host Dr. Darren welcomes AI experts Lynn Comp from Intel and Russell Fisher from NetApp to dive into the intricacies of Enterprise AI. Key points discussed include the misconceptions surrounding the costs of implementing AI solutions, the challenges of integrating various data types, and the importance of building a resilient, enterprise-grade environment for AI deployment.
