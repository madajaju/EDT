In this exciting episode of "Embracing Digital Transformation," host Dr. Darren welcomes Dinesh Soomro, founder and CEO of Bookkeeper AI, to explore the transformative potential of AI for small businesses. Dinesh shares his journey from Pakistan to Canada and how his passion for technology led him to create innovative AI-driven solutions, such as bookkeeping AI agents that shift from merely generating responses to executing meaningful actions.
