Embracing Change: How to Stay Relevant and Avoid Obsolescence

Dr. Darren welcomes Alister Frost, a seasoned change evangelist and author, to discuss the pivotal themes of Change, obsolescence, and personal missions in the workplace. Frost shares his unique journey from working in manufacturing at Kimberly Clark to the tech world at Microsoft, emphasizing how his experiences shaped his understanding of the essential need for continuous improvement and adaptation in an ever-changing landscape. Throughout the episode, Frost reveals his framework, known as the "FROST model," which outlines how individuals and organizations can adopt a culture of Change through openness, curiosity, and mission-driven work.
