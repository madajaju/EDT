Navigating Change in Established Industries

In this episode of "Embracing Digital Transformation," host Dr. Darren welcomes Lindsay Phillips, founder of SkyPhi Studios, to discuss the crucial topic of navigating change in established industries. With a unique background that transitioned from technical writing to strategic change management, Lindsay shares key insights into the complexities organizations face when rolling out new technologies. The conversation highlights the critical role of the human aspect in change management and provides practical strategies for bridging the gap between technology and people.
