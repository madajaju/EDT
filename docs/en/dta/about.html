About Digital Transformation Architect (DTA)

Establishing the Architectural Canon

DTA is the authoritative series within the Embracing Digital ecosystem, providing leaders with a stable, repeatable, and defensible framework for complex change.

Why This Series Exists

Digital transformation is plagued by competing definitions and vendor-driven hype. Digital Transformation Architect exists to establish a stable architectural canon: shared language, repeatable decisions, and defensible tradeoffs grounded in the Open Digital Transformation Architecture (ODXA).

Hosted by Dr. Darren Pulsipher, the show moves beyond tools and weekly trends to examine how organizations design, govern, and sustain transformation across five critical domains: Strategic, Organizational, Process, Digital, and Physical.

This series is designed specifically for executives, architects, and public-sector leaders who are responsible for outcomes that must endure beyond a single technology cycle.
