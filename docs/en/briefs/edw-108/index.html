Empathy and Communication in AI

This week on Embracing Digital, Dr. Darren Pulsipher explores two crucial topics: the rise of empathetic AI and mastering communication in tech. AI innovator Artem Rodichev discusses how empathetic AI is addressing loneliness, the ethical challenges of AI companionship, and the need for responsible regulation. Meanwhile, Silicon Valley communication expert Loreta Tarozaite shares insights on overcoming common communication barriers in tech, the power of visual storytelling, and how generative AI can help craft effective messages without replacing authenticity. Whether you're interested in the future of AI or seeking to enhance your connections with others, this episode is packed with valuable insights.
