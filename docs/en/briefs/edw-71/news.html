New AI-Driven Ransomware Evades Detection

Researchers at Texas A&M University and Ho Technical University have developed a new ransomware attack method called Evolutional Generative Adversarial Network (EGAN). This approach uses advanced AI to create ransomware capable of evading many commercial antivirus systems. By combining evolution strategies and GANs, the ransomware can mutate and appear benign, making detection challenging. The findings highlight the need for more robust cybersecurity measures to counter these sophisticated threats.
