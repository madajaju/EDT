In Edge Computing news

"Rakuten Launches Next-Gen Edge Computing Station"

Japanese giant Rakuten Inc. has unveiled its next-generation Edge Computing base station. This station, powered by the combined force of 5G and AI technology, is set to revolutionize IoT applications. The use of 5 G ensures high-speed data transfer, while AI enhances the station's processing power. This milestone is critical for Rakuten's business ecosystem, demonstrating a significant advance in fostering IoT and digital innovation. With unrivaled speed and processing power, this new tool could revolutionize the IoT industry, offering greater efficiency and connectivity for devices and applications.

"Urgent Call for IoT Cybersecurity Overhaul"

Industry executives are raising the alarm about the growing risk of botnet infections precipitated by vulnerabilities in Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Many of these devices remain insecure and poorly protected, making them prime targets for cyber threats. These circumstances showcase a glaring need for immediate and all-encompassing cybersecurity measures. Vigorous and efficient security protocols must be implemented without delay to curtail the persistent danger from botnets and provide a safe digital environment.

"Revolutionizing IoT: Energy-Efficient Backscatter Communications"

Pusan National University has developed a proposal for backscatter communications within low-power Internet of Things (IoT) devices. This innovative idea offers a potential solution to power management issues facing the rapidly growing edge-computing environment. If successful, the proposed backscatter communication system would significantly reduce the energy consumption of IoT devices, making them more sustainable and efficient. This reduction in energy consumption could lead to longer battery life and less frequent recharging, a critical improvement needed in this era of advanced technology and increased connectivity.

Cybersecurity

In Cybersecurity news

In Cybersecurity news

"Urgent Warnings: Rising Cyber Threats from China"

Various global cybersecurity agencies are issuing alarming warnings about a heightened cyber threat from China. These agencies caution organizations to bolster their defense systems against potential cyberattacks. This rise in cyber threats emphasizes the growing need for rigorous cybersecurity measures. Threatening activities reportedly include data theft, ransomware attacks, and corporate espionage, underscoring the urgency for organizations to protect their digital infrastructures promptly and efficiently.

"Cybersecurity Concerns Loom for Paris Olympics"

As the Paris Olympics approach, cybersecurity experts are bracing for potential difficulties. They anticipate substantial hurdles in safeguarding the event's digital infrastructure from possible threats. Many digital assets are vulnerable, from online ticketing systems to broadcasting networks and athletes' data. Cybercriminals could exploit weaknesses in the digital framework, causing disruption, misinformation, or theft. The need for robust cybersecurity measures is critical to maintaining the smooth flow of the event.

"AT&T Cyberattack Exposes Data Breach Risks"

AT&T recently experienced a cyberattack, resulting in a significant data breach. In this breach, customers' call logs were compromised, highlighting the persisting issues surrounding network cybersecurity. This situation underscores the need for stronger cybersecurity measures in leading telecom companies. In this era where data is gold, such security misdemeanors expose customers to potential breaches of privacy and identity theft. It further underscores the need for stronger cybersecurity measures to protect users' sensitive data.

"OpenAI and Microsoft: Redefining AI Together"

OpenAI and tech giant Microsoft have embarked on a promising collaboration that could redefine AI research and optimize the GPT platform. The core objective of this partnership is to investigate and develop safe and potentially beneficial applications of artificial intelligence in line with global demands. The alliance anticipates significantly influencing AI's transformative role in society by prioritizing safety and the broad implications of artificial general intelligence.

"AI's Energy Demand: A Sustainability Crisis"

A recent study has raised alarm over the significant amounts of electricity Artificial Intelligence consumes, which is adding strain to the power grid. This environmental impact worries experts, who are now calling for a shift towards more sustainable practices in AI development. They emphasize that the field needs to focus on reducing energy consumption to address rising environmental concerns, demonstrating a more significant commitment to sustainability to reduce its carbon footprint.

"Ensuring Fairness in AI Deployment"

The Department of Justice recently convened an interagency conference on promoting equality in the utilization of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Participants engaged in detailed discussions about AI deployment strategies and crucial measures needed to reduce bias in AI systems and ensure their fair and unbiased usage. The gathering emphasized the importance of implementing AI technologies in a manner that upholds principles of equality and fairness across different sectors.
