This Week at a Glance

This week in digital transformation, significant developments include the establishment of a 1 million-strong cyber workforce, various cloud service outages, and advancements in artificial intelligence with the integration of AI-generated code into the C++ standard library.

These events highlight critical trends in cybersecurity, cloud reliability, and the evolving role of AI in software development, impacting businesses’ operational integrity and innovation potential.
