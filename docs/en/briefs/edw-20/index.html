This Week at a Glance

In the week of June 19, 2023, digital transformation news highlighted key developments affecting the workforce, technology infrastructure, and regulatory landscape.

The ongoing trend of managers substituting human roles with AI indicates a significant shift in labor dynamics. Furthermore, recurring cloud outages raise concerns about system reliability, while new privacy laws challenge major tech companies' operations.
