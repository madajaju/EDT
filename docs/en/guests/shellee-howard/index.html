Shellee Howard

Biography

I help students and families navigate the college admissions process so they can get into the right college without losing their mind or their money.

As a founder, college strategist, and certified college consultant, I guide families through one of the biggest academic, financial, and career decisions of their lives. My work is focused on helping students stand out, identify best-fit colleges, choose the right path, and graduate with as little debt as possible.

My journey into this work started with my own children. After helping my son gain admission to Harvard and secure multiple full scholarships, I realized most families were making life-changing college decisions without a clear strategy. That experience led me back to school to become a certified college consultant and ultimately to found College Ready.

Today, my team and I help middle school and high school students build what we call a Stand Out Strategy — a personalized plan designed to help each student find the right academic, social, athletic, and financial fit for college.

At College Ready, we help families: • build a clear college admissions strategy • position students to stand out in a competitive applicant pool • identify best-fit colleges • navigate scholarships and merit aid opportunities • choose majors and future paths with intention • reduce stress, confusion, and costly mistakes

Over the years, our students have earned significant scholarships, multiple college acceptances, and admission to top universities. I am an international speaker, was named the 2026 Global Educational Consultant of the Year, recognized as one of the 100 Women to Know, and have been featured on FOX, CBS, and NBC for my work in college admissions, scholarships, and student success. I also appeared on Next Level CEO with Daymond John, where I shared my mission to help families make smarter, more strategic decisions about college and the future.

I am passionate about education, service, and helping students build meaningful futures. Through College Ready and our nonprofit efforts, we also support education initiatives internationally, including helping build schools and expand opportunities for underserved students.

If you are a parent of a middle school or high school student and want a clearer strategy for college admissions, scholarships, and long-term fit, let’s connect.
