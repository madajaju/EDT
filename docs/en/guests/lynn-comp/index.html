LinkedIn Profile

I am a global strategy, marketing, and product management executive with a passion for creating and delivering technology solutions to unlock new business value. During my time as the VP of Marketing, Server Business Unit at AMD, I led the sales enablement and marketing for the EPYC product line, resulting in industry-leading revenue growth. Currently, I am the Global Head of Sales for the AI Center of Excellence at Intel and a board member at NeuReality, a startup that develops AI hardware solutions to accelerate the AI accelerators.

My expertise in AI, cloud, enterprise IT, software, 5G, product marketing, ecosystem development, and strategy has shaped the vision and direction of the companies I work for. I have been a CHIEF member and a recipient of the 2020 Women of Technology award by Connected World. I believe in dignifying each individual with integrity, collaboration, and mentorship. I am inspired by leaders who navigate situations from zero experience, such as Jack Ma, and I enjoy taking on intrapreneurial and entrepreneurial roles that bridge skills or domains for new market entry and growth initiatives.

Small Language Models: The Public Gen AI Killer?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) isn't just a buzzword—it's reshaping how businesses operate. Join host Dr. Darren, a seasoned enterprise architect, alongside Lynn Comp, an expert in innovative Data Center technology trends, as they dive into the current landscape of AI and how organizations can leverage it to thrive. In this conversation, they tackle the complexities of AI benchmarks, use cases, and the future of AI enterprise architecture. ## Key Takeaways: - **Understand AI Benchmarks**: Recognize that metrics such as tokens per second don't always translate to business value; actionability matters more than speed. - **Start Small**: Experimenting with small, manageable AI projects can yield significant insights without overhauling existing infrastructure. - **Focus on Business Needs**: Tailor AI implementations to your industry’s specific use cases to enhance operational efficiency and reduce liability. - **Embrace Hybrid Architectures**: Leverage both on-premise and cloud solutions to create a robust AI framework that mitigates risks like outages and data breaches. - **Upskill Your Workforce**: Prepare your team for the AI-driven future by fostering critical thinking skills and data literacy. ## Chapters: - 00:00 Introduction and Hook - 02:30 AI Benchmarks: Understanding the Metrics - 05:15 Use Cases for AI in Business - 08:00 The Importance of Starting Small - 10:45 The Role of Hybrid Architectures - 14:30 Upskilling Your Workforce for AI - 17:00 Recommendations and Next Steps - 19:00 Conclusion and Call to Action Join us in this enlightening episode that encourages technologists and business leaders to embrace change and harness the potential of AI in their operations.
