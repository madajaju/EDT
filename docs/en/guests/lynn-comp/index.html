LinkedIn Profile

I am a global strategy, marketing, and product management executive with a passion for creating and delivering technology solutions to unlock new business value. During my time as the VP of Marketing, Server Business Unit at AMD, I led the sales enablement and marketing for the EPYC product line, resulting in industry-leading revenue growth. Currently, I am the Global Head of Sales for the AI Center of Excellence at Intel and a board member at NeuReality, a startup that develops AI hardware solutions to accelerate the AI accelerators.

My expertise in AI, cloud, enterprise IT, software, 5G, product marketing, ecosystem development, and strategy has shaped the vision and direction of the companies I work for. I have been a CHIEF member and a recipient of the 2020 Women of Technology award by Connected World. I believe in dignifying each individual with integrity, collaboration, and mentorship. I am inspired by leaders who navigate situations from zero experience, such as Jack Ma, and I enjoy taking on intrapreneurial and entrepreneurial roles that bridge skills or domains for new market entry and growth initiatives.

From Hype to Impact: Building Scalable AI Solutions for the Enterprise

In this episode of "Embracing Digital Transformation," host Dr. Darren welcomes AI experts Lynn Comp from Intel and Russell Fisher from NetApp to dive into the intricacies of Enterprise AI. They explore how organizations can harness the power of AI without compromising their intellectual property and data security. Key points discussed include the misconceptions surrounding the costs of implementing AI solutions, the challenges of integrating various data types, and the importance of building a resilient, enterprise-grade environment for AI deployment. The discussions reveal valuable insights and actionable strategies that leaders in technology and business can use to navigate the evolving landscape of artificial intelligence effectively. ## Takeaways - Understand the difference between experimental AI and enterprise AI, focusing on delivering real value. - Learn about the collaboration between Intel and NetApp to create accessible and reliable AI solutions. - Discover the importance of protecting intellectual property while using AI tools within organizations. - Recognize the role of open-source platforms in enhancing AI capabilities and driving innovation. - Explore real-world use cases that highlight the benefits of integrating AI in healthcare and other industries. ## Chapters - **00:01 - Introduction to the Episode** - **02:30 - Lynn Russell's Background and Expertise** - **05:15 - Misconceptions about AI Implementation Costs** - **10:45 - The Need for Enterprise AI Solutions** - **15:30 - Collaboration Between Intel and NetApp** - **20:00 - Protection of Intellectual Property in AI Usage** - **25:00 - Open Source Platforms and their Role in AI** - **30:00 - Real Case Studies of Enterprise AI Applications** - **35:00 - Future Directions in the AI Landscape**
