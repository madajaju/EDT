Hernan Lonodo

Biography

I am an accomplished Information Technology executive and Cybersecurity professional with over two decades of experience. With a career spanning close to 30 years, I have consistently demonstrated my ability to adapt and excel in various segments of the technology landscape. Over the 15 years, I held dual roles as Chief Technology Officer and Chief Information Security Officer, emphasizing my capacity to lead and strategize in diverse areas of the field.

Throughout my journey, I have made significant contributions in the realms of Higher Education and Healthcare, which have not only led to a long and successful tenure at my previous job but have also allowed me to expand my influence by serving as the chair of Boards of Directors for charitable organizations. Additionally, I've provided valuable advisory services to Silicon Valley cybersecurity startups and local IT/Cybersecurity services companies.

In my most recent role, I have embraced a new challenge as the lead strategist for the education vertical at one of the world's top 50 companies. As a key player in this capacity, my responsibilities encompass formulating cutting-edge strategies for digital transformation and innovation tailored to colleges and universities. Internally, I serve as a trusted advisor to our company's leadership, providing invaluable insights on how to position our products and services effectively within the education market.

My expertise extends to innovation, AI, cybersecurity, and leadership, making me a recognized subject matter expert in these areas. Beyond my professional endeavors, I am also a prolific writer and public speaker, with a track record of published articles in prominent IT and Cybersecurity trade magazines and a history of speaking at national conferences. My speaking engagements have covered a wide range of topics, from disruptive thinking to mitigating industry-related crises, halting network cyberattacks, and exploring the power of integrations to foster a culture of digital transformation.

This evolving journey reflects my commitment to staying at the forefront of IT and Cybersecurity, enabling me to make a lasting impact on the education sector and continue sharing my insights with the broader community.
