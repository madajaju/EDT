LinkedIn Profile

Luv Kapur has worked as a Software Engineering Consultant (R&D) at Bit (bit.dev) since December 2021. Prior to this, Luv worked at HOOPP (Healthcare of Ontario Pension Plan) from 2017 to December 2021. During their time at HOOPP, Luv held various roles including Solutions Architect, Senior Systems Developer, Product Owner, Associate Systems Developer, and Systems Developer. Luv was part of an investment technology team responsible for building applications and services to automate and improve investment decision-making, operational efficiency, and reduce operational risk. They were responsible for full stack development, designing and developing custom front-office applications in an agile environment. Luv's key accomplishments included leading the frontend architecture and design of a liquidity management and trading platform, maintaining and enhancing an in-house custom equity trading application by adding analytics and compliance checks, and improving user experience by optimizing performance and compliance validation.

Luv Kapur's education history begins in 2011 when they attended St. Francis De Sales School in New Delhi, India. Luv spent two years there, from 2011 to 2013, and completed their high school education with a focus on Computer Science.

After completing high school, Luv went on to pursue further education at Seneca College from 2013 to 2017. During this four-year period, they obtained an Honours Bachelor of Technology degree (B.Tech.) in Software Development.

Based on the provided information, Luv Kapur's education history includes completing high school with a focus on Computer Science at St. Francis De Sales School and obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Software Development from Seneca College.
