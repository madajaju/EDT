Shirish Nimgaonkar

Shirish Nimgaonkar

Shirish is an entrepreneur, advisor to and investor in multiple technology companies, with a focus on software and AI. He is currently the Founder and CEO of an AI enabled predictive automation software company. In the past, he has been a part of the senior management teams of multiple VC backed entrepreneurial companies with significant growth and successful exits. Shirish was also a Managing Director at a global investment bank, where he advised several leading high growth technology companies across US, Europe and Asia on this growth strategy, mergers and acquisitions, capital raising and new business development. Shirish has advised multiple startups in areas such as SaaS, AI and B2B software across functions such as solution / product development, sales, marketing, team building, capital raising and acquisitions.

Shirish has a B.Tech from the Indian Institute of Technology - Bombay, MS from Stanford University (ITC Scholar) and MBA from the Harvard Business School. Trained in classical music, Shirish is also the founder and lead singer of a music band focused on social good.

The Future of Work: AI's Role in IT Management

In this episode of "Embracing Digital Transformation," host Dr. Darren welcomes special guest Shirish Nimgaonkar, founder and CEO of eBlissAI, to explore the topic "The Future of Work: AI's Role in IT Management." This insightful discussion delves into how AI is transforming the workplace, with a specific focus on the complexities of device management. Together, they investigate the balance between automation and human intervention, emphasizing the need for effective diagnosis and root cause analysis in device management.
