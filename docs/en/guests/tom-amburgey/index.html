Tom Amburgey

Biography

Leveraging my faith and experience, I currently lead Euna Solutions, a GovTech software company that helps Public Sector agencies manage the full lifecycle of grants, budgets, procurement, and payments. We serve thousands of agencies across the U.S. and Canada.

Before Euna, I spent four years as CEO at Civix(previously GCR), and before that I led the $175M Public Administration business at CentralSquare Technologies through a period of double-digit growth, multiple acquisitions, and a full platform transformation. Earlier in my career, I came up through IT leadership in local government — which is why I understand our customers the way I do.

I’ve been involved in M&A from every angle: sourcing targets, running diligence, negotiating terms, and doing the hard work of integration. Most recently we acquired GrantExec to bring AI-powered grant discovery into our platform. I’ve also been through the debt-raising process, helping put together significantly oversubscribed offerings.

What drives me: building great teams, creating real value for customers, and leaving every organization better than I found it. My Team and I Get Things Done.
