Business transformation: I have result-oriented leadership experience in designing public-private partnership programs, enabling business and reputation, policy advocacy, new models for inclusive innovation by bringing in business, technology innovation, and development agendas together for the tech MNC and ecosystem growth.

Thought leader and respected speaker: I am passionate about technology innovation and changing skills - jobs, youth empowerment, education transformation, reducing inequality.
