Santosh Kaveti

Biography

Santosh Kaveti is a technologist, entrepreneur, and visionary leader with a passion for driving meaningful transformation. As the CEO and Founder of ProArch, he has built a purpose-driven company rooted in innovation, integrity, and impact. Under his leadership, ProArch has grown into a trusted technology partner for enterprises worldwide—delivering strategic consulting and solutions across cloud and infrastructure, AI and data analytics, cybersecurity and compliance, application development, and quality assurance.

Santosh believes that technology should not only solve problems but also inspire progress. His holistic, people-first approach ensures that ProArch’s solutions align with long-term business goals while empowering teams to thrive. His global perspective—shaped by traveling to over 44 countries—continues to influence how he sees the future: interconnected, human-centric, and full of opportunity.

At the heart of his mission is the desire to create lasting value—not just for clients, but for communities. As the founder of the Yashoda Foundation, a volunteer-led nonprofit, Santosh champions causes that uplift underprivileged children, support struggling families, and raise social awareness. His work reflects a core belief: real success is measured by how many lives you improve along the way.

Beyond the boardroom, Santosh is a dedicated family man. Based in Atlanta, Georgia—where ProArch is headquartered—he finds balance in time spent with his wife and children, whether it's a spontaneous outing, quiet moments at home, or exploring the outdoors together. He often travels to India to reconnect with family, and when time allows, escapes to Costa Rica to tend to his citrus farm—a personal sanctuary and symbol of sustainable living.

As an investor, mentor, and Chairman of the Board at Enhops (a ProArch company), Santosh remains committed to nurturing the next generation of innovators. His journey is defined not just by business achievements, but by a deep sense of purpose, curiosity, and care.
