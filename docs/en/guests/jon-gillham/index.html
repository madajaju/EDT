Biography

Jon Gillham is an experienced entrepreneur and innovator in the digital content world. As the Founder and CEO of Originality.ai, he’s focused on solving one of the biggest challenges in the industry today—ensuring the authenticity of online content.

With over a decade in SEO and content marketing, Jon’s journey started by managing a portfolio of content sites. His passion for the industry led him to launch and successfully sell two content marketing agencies, proving his knack for staying ahead of industry trends.

Jon is genuinely excited by how quickly things are changing in the digital content space, and he’s constantly looking for ways to adapt and innovate to meet the needs of this fast-moving field.
