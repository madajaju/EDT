Dr. Darren Pulsipher

Chief Enterprise Architect, Public Sector, Intel Corporation · Innovator · Author · Host of Embracing Digital

LinkedIn Profile

Dr. Darren Pulsipher is the Chief Enterprise Architect for Public Sector at Intel Corporation, where he helps government and enterprise leaders align people, process, and technology to drive real digital transformation. Drawing on decades of executive and engineering experience, he brings a rigorous systems approach to complex missions—translating technical innovation into clear strategy and measurable outcomes.

He is the host of the award-winning podcasts Embracing Digital Transformation and Embracing Digital This Week, where he speaks with global experts, policymakers, and industry pioneers to unpack what’s really changing in AI, cloud, cybersecurity, and modern infrastructure. The shows are produced in eight languages and reach more than 500,000 downloads each month.

Beyond the studio, Dr. Pulsipher serves as an Adjunct Professor of Computer Science at Vanderbilt University and chairs The Open Group’s Government Enterprise Architecture Working Group, leading the development of the Open Digital Transformation Architecture (ODXA).

A prolific author and speaker, he has written three books and over 100 articles on technology and transformation, and holds eight patents in cloud and grid computing architectures—work that has helped organizations modernize how they build, deploy, and operate critical systems.
