Technology Dimension

Platforms and Data

The Digital Enabler

The Technology dimension provides the digital infrastructure and software capabilities that enable the transformation. While technology is the most visible element of change, its success depends on its integration with people, processes, and policies.

Platform Strategy

Selecting and building the digital foundations (Cloud, Edge, IoT) that support the organization's mission. Ensuring that technical stacks are resilient, scalable, and secure.

Data Models & Pipelines

Defining how information is structured, governed, and moved across the enterprise. Enabling the data-driven insights required for AI and advanced decision-making.

Technical Execution

The practical application of the Execution Pillars (AI, Cyber, Computing, etc.) to solve specific mission challenges. Moving from pilot projects to enterprise-scale capabilities.

Key Considerations

  • Interoperability: Ensuring that disparate systems and data sources can communicate and share information seamlessly.
  • Technical Debt Management: Proactively addressing legacy systems that impede innovation and increase operational risk.
  • Security by Design: Integrating Zero Trust principles and resilient patterns into every layer of the technology stack.

Technology in the GEAR System

Within GEAR, the Technology dimension is primarily addressed through the Digital Domain of O-DXA and the six Execution Pillars. The Enhance stage of FORGE is where technology is leveraged to augment existing capabilities and drive the "Architect's Explosion."
