What is ODXA?

ODXA is a domain-based architecture that helps organizations reason about transformation decisions across interacting concerns. It provides a stable way to identify responsibilities, reveal tradeoffs, and connect decisions to outcomes.

The five domains

  • Strategic — intent, outcomes, investment, risk, and tradeoffs
  • Organizational — decision ownership, accountability, structure, and incentives
  • Process — value flow, execution, governance mechanisms, and measurable performance
  • Digital — platforms, data, applications, integration, and operational capabilities
  • Physical — infrastructure, networks, devices, resilience, and real-world constraints

How to use ODXA

Start with the domain most impacted by your initiative, then trace dependencies into other domains. Use the domain pages as reference hubs and the episode pages for deep guidance and examples.
