Domain definition

The Process Domain encompasses the workflows, governance mechanisms, and operational routines of the system. It describes how activities are orchestrated to transform strategic intent into tangible public services while managing risk and compliance across the **Transformation Dimensions**.

Key decisions

  • How do we automate workflows to improve service delivery?
  • What governance controls are necessary for legislative compliance?
  • How are risks identified, analyzed, and mitigated across operations?
  • What support processes ensure business continuity and disaster recovery?

Common anti-patterns

  • Process Bloat (excessive bureaucracy and "red tape")
  • Shadow Processes (workarounds created to bypass broken formal systems)
  • Compliance as a Checkbox (treating regulations as a hurdle rather than a value)

Cross-domain dependencies

  • Strategic Domain
  • Digital Domain
  • Physical Domain

GEAR Integration & Architect's Map

The Process Domain is the engine of the GEAR system, where strategic intent is transformed into operational execution.

FORGE Methodology in Process

Architects use FORGE to refine and optimize the flows that deliver mission value.

Stage Architect's Focus Key Artifacts
Find Identify "swivel-chair" processes, manual handoffs, and shadow workflows. Process Inventory, Value Stream Map
Observe Measure actual cycle times, error rates, and process friction points. Performance Baseline Report
Reconcile Unify disparate workflows into a coherent enterprise delivery engine. Standardized Workflow Design
Ground Root process changes in existing operational realities and compliance rules. Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
Enhance Augment throughput via AI-driven automation and continuous feedback loops. Automated Pipeline Blueprint

Process Dimensions Map

How the Four Dimensions are orchestrated within the Process Domain.

Dimension Process Play Example Check
People Defining roles within the workflow and behavioral feedback. Do users understand their role in the new automated flow?
Process The core lifecycle and feedback loops of the enterprise. Is the feedback loop closed or just producing logs?
Policy Embedding compliance and risk controls into the flow. Are regulatory gates automated or manual blockers?
Technology The tools that orchestrate and monitor the process. Does our BPM/workflow engine scale with demand?

Pillar-Domain Intersection

Architect's checklist for aligning execution pillars with the Process Domain.

Pillar Process Requirement Verification Point
AI Integrate model feedback into the operational flow. Verify AI triggers for automated human escalation.
Cyber Embed continuous monitoring and automated response. Check if incident playbooks are tested and current.
Data Standardize "DataOps" quality gates at every step. Ensure data capture doesn't slow down the value stream.
Edge Define local "Store and Forward" sync processes. Verify site-local failover process continuity.
Computing Automate environment scaling and CI/CD pipelines. Check for manual approvals in the release process.
Comms Establish traffic prioritization based on process criticality. Verify low-bandwidth process degradation paths.
